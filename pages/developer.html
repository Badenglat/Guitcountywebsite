About Me

I am a dedicated software engineer who loves building digital tools to help communities. I focus on using technology to make a positive impact, especially in South Sudan's digital development.

Creating the Guit County portal has been about making local government more transparent and accessible. By building a single platform for news, services, and administration, I'm improving how citizens interact with their government in Guit County, Unity State.

My Vision

Digital sovereignty begins at the county level. By giving local governments strong, secure, and easy-to-use platforms, I help citizens become more informed and engaged, connecting old traditions with the digital future.
